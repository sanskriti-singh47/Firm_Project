Review Process


FIRMS Journals - follows quick evaluation method. In order to substantially reduce the time to publication as compared with other journals. After submission, all articles result will announce as soon as possible ( not later than 2 months). Editorial board members will make the final decision. Submit the original article without any plagiarism and copyright material.Each paper will be reviewed by at least two experts and maximum five. The submission will be sent out to review on the same day. Reviewers will return the review comments after receiving the paper.
The Report will be sent to authors on the same day when received by reviewer as per decision. Final document will be evaluated by technical committee whether author modified the document as per reviewer suggestion. If satisfied document will forward to publication committee otherwise once again document will forward to author for modification. Periodical update will happen within a month.
